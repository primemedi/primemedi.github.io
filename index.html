IRF

RF-Based ICE cooling Body Slimming System

RF System

Generally, fat begins to dissolve at 41°C or above.
The bonding structure of fat tissue in obese people tends to be more solid than those who are not, and their solid fat tissues usually retain unnecessary water throughout the tissues.
Deep heat produced by high frequency wave is helpful in loosening the bonding structure of these solid fat tissues and also dissolving water between the tissues to accelerate the fat tissues burning.

Principle of ICE Therapy

Cryo thermo Therapy : Based on Human Homeostasis System. When the body temperature drops. blood circulation is triggered to maintain homeostasis.
During this process, body burns fat cells to use it as fuel to increase body temperature.

Cooling Effect

It is known that our body works most actively when body temperature goes down by 8°C from a normal body temperature 36.5°C. STYLE equipped with ICE Cooling System effectively lowers a temperature through a powerful ice-cooling process to drop more than 8 degrees, by applying the handle to the body part.

Cooling Treatment

It increase the absorptance of the penetrated medication by stimulating the cells of the cooled skin surface with a powerful cooling performance to drop max. 20°C, it also reduces a risk of downtime and PIH after the procedure.

Penetration of Solution

Electric pulse of STYLE-EPI makes micro-sized holes temporarily on the skin surface by disturbing phospholipid double membranes which make up the cell membranes and letting the over-40,000 daltons of solution penetrate inside the cells.
